    Mini NodeCopter

    Ever wanted to play with a drone on a boat?

    As we're gathering an amazing hacker crowd for the Realtime Conference Europe, we thought we should get them to play with Drones and show us the amazing hacks they've been building.

    The party will be held on La Plateforme, from 8PM to 1AM on Monday April 22nd. There will be drinks (non alcoholic though). However you're more than welcome to start working on your hacks before as we will only have 5 hours.

    Get a ticket!

    Hacks

    The folks at Superfeedr will bring 4 Parrot AR Drones for the NodeCopter hackers, but you are also more than welcome to bring your own.

    We are also looking forward to seeing people bring their Arduino, their RaspberryPi and all other kinds of hack they've been working on.

    Finally, there will be prizes! An jury will determine the best hacks for the night and will grant these hackers prizes from Github, among others!
